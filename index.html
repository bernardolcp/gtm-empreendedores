GTM for Entrepreneurs

From founder intuition to repeatable demand.

A practical go-to-market workshop for founders who need to find customers, learn fast, and build a motion that survives beyond the founder.

Workshop deckEnglish version
01 · Definition

GTM is not marketing. It is the system that turns a painful problem into repeated sales.

1

Who

The specific customer segment you can reach, understand, and serve first.

2

Why now

The urgent pain, event, or constraint that makes action more likely today.

3

How they buy

The channel, message, trust mechanism, and sales motion that create conversion.

Key ideaGTM is an operating system, not a campaign.
02 · The founder trap

Most founders start with a product story. Customers respond to a problem story.

Product story

“We built a platform that uses AI to optimize operations.”

Usually too abstract, too broad, and too early.

Problem story

“Your team loses three hours every day copying customer information across five tools.”

Specific pain creates urgency, trust, and a buying conversation.

RuleSell the painful before the possible.
03 · Start with a wedge

Your first market should be narrow enough to dominate, not broad enough to impress.

Segment

Pick one customer type.

Pain

Pick one expensive problem.

Moment

Find when the pain becomes urgent.

Channel

Pick the fastest path to reach them.

Proof

Show one concrete outcome.

Workshop exerciseWrite your wedge in one sentence.
04 · ICP

An ICP is not a persona. It is a buying condition.

Bad ICP

“Small businesses that want to grow.”

Better ICP

“Owner-led home-service businesses spending on leads but losing follow-up on WhatsApp.”

Best ICP

“Owner-led service businesses with active ads, missed leads, and no CRM discipline.”

TestCan you find 50 of them this week?
05 · Message

The message should make the customer feel seen before it makes you sound smart.

“If your customer would not repeat the sentence to a peer, it is not your message yet.”

Message formula

For [specific customer]
who [painful situation]
we help [clear outcome]
without [current friction]

OutputOne sentence, no jargon.
06 · Channels

Choose channels based on behavior, not preference.

Outbound

Best when the ICP is identifiable and the pain is urgent.

Content

Best when trust, education, or category creation matters.

Paid

Best when intent already exists and conversion can be measured.

Partnerships

Best when another trusted player already owns access.

RuleStart with one primary channel and one learning channel.
07 · Sales motion

Early sales is not persuasion. It is diagnosis, proof, and next step control.

Trigger

Why talk now?

Diagnosis

Where does the pain show up?

Impact

What does it cost?

Proof

Why believe you?

Next step

What happens after this call?

Founder salesLearn before you scale.
08 · Metrics

Track the few numbers that reveal where the motion breaks.

Acquisition

Leads, source, cost per lead, reply rate.

Conversion

Meeting rate, close rate, sales cycle, activation.

Quality

Retention, referrals, payback, customer outcome.

Metric rule: If you cannot explain what you would change based on the metric, do not make it the headline.
Do not drown in dashboardsMeasure decisions.
09 · 30-day GTM sprint

A simple sprint to move from opinion to evidence.

Week 1 · Clarity

Define ICP, pain, wedge, offer, and first message.

Week 2 · Conversations

Run 20-30 customer conversations or outbound tests.

Week 3 · Offer

Turn learning into a sharper promise, package, and proof asset.

Week 4 · Repeatability

Double down on the strongest channel, document the motion, and decide what to stop doing.

GoalEvidence, not vanity activity.
10 · AI operator layer

AI helps most when it sits around the GTM workflow, not above it.

Research

Find accounts, signals, competitors, and objections.

Personalization

Draft context-aware outreach without losing human judgment.

CRM discipline

Summarize calls, update records, and trigger follow-ups.

Learning loop

Review what converts, what stalls, and what should change.

ImportantAI accelerates a motion. It does not replace market contact.
11 · Closing
GTM is learned in contact with the market.

The job is not to guess perfectly. It is to build a system that gets smarter every week: sharper ICP, clearer pain, stronger message, better channel, tighter proof.

Leave withone wedge, one channel, one offer, one 30-day experiment.
